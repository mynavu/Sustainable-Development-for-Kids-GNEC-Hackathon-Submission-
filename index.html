How you can help

We offer a wide variety of education materials that are fun and education for all kids to enjoy! You can donate directly to non-profit organizations to contribute to causes you care about.
Or, you can play games that educates you on global issues. Every game played will contribute to good causes. If you wish to learn more about the 17 Sustainable Development Goals
press "About" below. Alternatively, if you want to play games that are enjoyable and insightful, press "Games". We currently offer two games that teaches kids the importance of the 17
Sustainable Development Goals, which kids can play to help spread awareness. Lastly, you can press the "Donate" sign, to donate or learn about the missions of non-profit organizations.
All organizations are contribute to at least on of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. However, do not feel pressured to donate. There are many ways to contribute, doing research on
each organization and spreading the word is a great start. Have fun on this web app!
